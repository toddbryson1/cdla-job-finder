# CDLA.jobs — Carrier Brief

**One-page summary for recruiting directors, hiring managers, and fleet owners.**

---

## What we are

**CDLA.jobs is a CDL-A driver matching service, not a job board.** You don&rsquo;t post jobs on our platform. We take an API feed of the openings already published on your careers page, AI-prescreen drivers against your hiring criteria, and deliver the matches that clear your filters directly into your ATS — Tenstreet by default, integration configured by us.

The product is the prescreening. Drivers who don&rsquo;t match your stated criteria — location, experience, equipment, MVR disclosures, criminal history, endorsements, schedule preference — never reach your inbox. Drivers who do match are people the driver themself selected to share their prequalification with. Not a panel. Not a lead list resold to ten carriers.

---

## How it works

1. **Your jobs feed in.** As long as your careers page lists current openings, drivers see those roles in their CDLA.jobs matches.
2. **Drivers complete one intake.** We capture experience, equipment, endorsements, regions, schedule, and stage-2 safety answers.
3. **AI matches against your criteria.** Only drivers who clear your filters surface for your roles.
4. **The driver picks you.** Driver-side consent is per-carrier — they release their prequalification to you specifically.
5. **Their prequalification lands in your ATS.** Your team picks it up in the Tenstreet workflow they already use.
6. **You complete DOT 391, FCRA-authorized background, MVR, and the hiring decision.** That all stays inside your existing process. We don&rsquo;t touch FCRA-regulated steps.

## What makes this different

- **Drivers consent per-carrier, not to a panel.** When a driver picks you, you&rsquo;re the only carrier who gets that prequalification. Lead vendors sell the same contact to ten buyers; we don&rsquo;t.
- **You handle FCRA, DOT 391, MVR, PSP, drug/alcohol verification, and the hire.** We don&rsquo;t touch the regulated parts. We&rsquo;re a matching service, not a CRA.
- **The pricing replaces job-board spend, not stacks on top of it.** Carriers running large fleets pay the same $2,500/month as small ones at Tier 1. Lower advertising spend leaves more room to pay drivers better.
- **Tenstreet integration is included.** Setup and configuration handled by us. Carriers on other ATS platforms receive matched leads by email; the matching itself works the same either way.

---

## What we don&rsquo;t do

- Background checks (yours, in your ATS)
- MVR or PSP pulls (yours)
- DOT 391 application (yours)
- Driver-side guarantees of hire (we&rsquo;re matching, you decide)
- Pre-hire phone screens (your recruiters)
- Selling the same driver to multiple carriers without their consent (ever)
